# Summary
A podcast where 4 (+1 moderator) AI models debate on topics related to humans. 

Features: 
1. A 4x4 matrix of each AI model's frustration/anger with each other is measured live across podcast session.

1.1 After a threshold of frustration of one model to another is achieved, that AI model will 'crash out'. The 2 models will then spiral into a back and forth debate, while others awkward watch or take sides and chime in, just as human podcasts progress.
   
1.2 Moderator doesn't do much to help (as human podcasts go). Eventually steps in and subsides the situation. The 2 AI models remain holding a grudge, high risk of initiating argument again later. 

2. Each AI model is given a personality. Eg, Groq is sarcastic.
